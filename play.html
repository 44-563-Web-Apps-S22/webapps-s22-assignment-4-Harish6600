The Impostures of Scapin

ACT TWO They are talking about weapons making and how many crown members are there and which item is useful

SCENE XII.-OCTAVE, LÉANDRE, SCAPIN.

OCT

Well, Scapin, have your plans been successful?

OCT

(_going away_)

Then I must die, Sir, for I could not live without Zerbinette.

OCT

Heyy! stop, stop; my goodness, how quick you are!

OCT

What can become of me?

OCT

(_to_ LÉANDRE)

But I could do nothing for you.

OCT

(_going away_)

Then I must die, Sir, for I could not live without Zerbinette.

OCT

Hallo! stop, stop; my goodness, how quick you are!
